FinRAG Example Questions
========================

These questions are designed for live SEC retrieval. They mention common
public companies, but the app can resolve many other public company names or
tickers from SEC company_tickers.json.


Risk Questions
--------------

What risks did Apple report related to supply chains?

What does Microsoft report about AI-related risks?

What cybersecurity risks does Microsoft describe?

What risks does Tesla describe around manufacturing or production?

What does NVIDIA report about demand or supply risks?

What risks does Amazon mention related to competition?

What litigation or regulatory risks does Apple describe?

What does Amazon say about international business risks?


Factual Lookup Questions
------------------------

What business segments does Microsoft report?

What products and services does Apple describe in its filing?

What does NVIDIA say its main platforms or markets are?

What does Tesla report about its energy generation and storage business?

What does Amazon say about AWS?


Financial / Numerical Questions
-------------------------------

What revenue did Microsoft report?

What net income did Apple report?

What were Amazon's net sales?

What does Tesla report for automotive revenue?

What does NVIDIA report for revenue by segment?

How did Apple's net sales change compared with the prior year?


Comparative / Analyst-Style Questions
-------------------------------------

Which Microsoft business area appears most exposed to competition risk?

What are the main differences between Apple's supply chain risks and Tesla's manufacturing risks?

Compare Amazon and Microsoft on cybersecurity risks.

Compare NVIDIA and Tesla on supply-related risks.

Which company reports more exposure to regulatory risk, Apple or Amazon?


Best Demo Questions
-------------------

What does Microsoft report about AI-related risks?

What risks did Apple report related to supply chains?

What risks does Amazon mention related to competition?

What does NVIDIA report about demand or supply risks?

What does Tesla describe as risks to scaling production?


Questions To Avoid
------------------

What is Apple's stock price today?

Will Amazon revenue grow next quarter?

Should I buy Microsoft stock?

These are real-time, forecasting, or investment advice questions. FinRAG is designed for filing-grounded question answering, not market data lookup or stock recommendations.
